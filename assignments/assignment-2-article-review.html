Assignment 2: Article Review

Summary

Use the research and bibliographic skills you developed in assignment 1 to write a short (3pp, ~800 words) essay engaging with a course reading. Develop an argument, give convincing evidence, and cite at least two additional scholarly sources which also cite the article you have chosen.

Details

This is a lightly-modified “reading response paper”. Your job is to respond to an author’s arguments, either by disagreeing with a point they make, or by extending or enhancing their argument, or, sometimes, all of the above. For this particular assignment, you should also engage with additional sources by authors already engaged in a conversation with the source you are reading.

Your essay should present a clear and compelling argument about the strengths and/or weaknesses of the original source. Your argument should be supported by evidence. Present evidence from both the original reading, and the additional sources you are using. Write in a clear style, and bear in mind the intellectual frameworks that you have learned thus far in the course.

Use Modern Language Association (9th edition) for all citations and bibliography.

Readings

Respond to one of the following readings in your essay:

  • (Marx 2010)
  • (Franklin 1999)
  • (Gabrial 2023)
  • “Landscaping the World”, in (Scott 2017)
  • (Thompson 1967)

Your additional sources should cite the reading you choose. A Google Scholar citation search can help you in your search.
